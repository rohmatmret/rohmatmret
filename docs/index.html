Privacy First Design

Your data stays on your device. Basketball Team Manager stores all your team information, player data, and statistics locally on your Android device. We do not collect or transmit your personal team data to our servers.

  • Team information stored locally only
  • Player data never leaves your device
  • Statistics stored on your device
  • Photos saved in private app storage
  • Encrypted password storage (AES256-GCM)
  • No cloud sync (complete privacy)
