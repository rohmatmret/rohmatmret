3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Data Storage

All your team data, player information, and statistics are stored locally on your device using:

Android Room Database:
  • SQLite database encrypted at rest (Android 6.0+)
  • Accessible only to the App
  • Protected by Android's app sandboxing
Encrypted Shared Preferences:
  • Account credentials (email, password) stored using Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences
  • Encrypted with AES256-GCM encryption
  • Key stored in Android Keystore (hardware-backed on supported devices)
Local File Storage:
  • Player photos stored in private app directory
  • Not accessible to other apps without permission

3.2 Data Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption at Rest: Credentials encrypted with AES256-GCM
  • Encryption in Transit: HTTPS for all network communications (ads, analytics)
  • App Sandboxing: Android's built-in app isolation
  • No Cloud Storage: Your personal data never leaves your device
  • Secure Coding Practices: Regular security reviews and updates

3.3 Data Backup

Android's Auto Backup feature may backup your App data to your Google account:

  • Controlled by your device settings
  • Encrypted by Google
  • Restored when you reinstall the App on a new device
You can disable Auto Backup in your device settings if preferred.

3.4 Third-Party Data Security

Data collected by Firebase and AdMob is protected by Google's security measures. See:

  • Google's Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Security

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access Your Data

All your data is stored locally on your device. You can access it anytime through the App's interface.

5.2 Delete Your Data

You can delete all your data at any time:

Option 1: Delete Account
  • Settings → Team Profile → Account → Delete Account
  • Permanently deletes all local data
Option 2: Uninstall App
  • Uninstalling the App removes all local data from your device
Note: Data backed up via Android Auto Backup may persist in your Google account. To delete:
  • Google Account → Data & Privacy → Delete a Google service → Delete backup data

5.3 Opt-Out of Analytics

You can limit analytics data collection:

  • Android Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Disable Firebase Analytics (this may affect app functionality)

5.4 Opt-Out of Personalized Ads

Android Settings:

1. Settings → Google → Ads

2. Toggle "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

3. Or tap "Reset advertising ID"

Note: You will still see ads, but they will be less relevant.

5.5 Revoke Permissions

You can revoke device permissions anytime:

  • Android Settings → Apps → Basketball Team Manager → Permissions
  • Toggle off Camera, Storage, or Contacts
Note: Some features may not work without required permissions.

5.6 Data Portability

Since all data is stored locally:

  • You have full access to your data
  • You can manually export data (future feature planned)
  • Android Auto Backup provides automatic backup

5.7 Do Not Track

The App does not respond to Do Not Track (DNT) signals, as we don't track your browsing activity across websites. Analytics are limited to in-app usage.
